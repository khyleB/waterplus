Our Mission

To provide clean water which will work towards improving health, and empower communities through:

1. Sustainable water infrastructure

2. Community-led initiatives

3. Education and training

4. Economic empowerment

How We Work

1. We engage with the community and learn of their assets and needs following the lead of the local leaders.

2. We engage with community members to identify solutions, discover leadership and build relationships.

3. Together, we realize the problem, assess water sources, and define a solution.

4. We aid in the acceptable solutions through supporting obtaining the necessary financial, technical and organizaiton al resources. . This empowers the community, particularly the women.

5. We empower communities to take ownership of maintenance and management committees as a collective responsibility.

Our Goal

Clean water changes lives:

1. Improved health and well-being (restoring Minobimaadziwn).

2. Increased education and economic opportunities.

3. Empowered women and children

4. Stronger, more resilient communities.
