Welcome to Water Plus

Water is Life

WaterPlus is a community-driven initiative providing sustainable water solutions to rural communities. Our mission is to empower women, children, and families and thus the whole community is enlivened through access to clean water, improving health, education, and economic opportunities.

WaterPlus addresses the root cause(s) of water scarcity in rural communities — often, whether directly or in indirectly, this root cause is colonization. Our collaborative approach brings together local leaders, organizations, and experts to suggest and support applicable sustainable water systems. We strive to do this with an indigenized perspective and by employing indigenous methodology.
