Welcome to WaterPlus

Water is Life

WaterPlus is a community-driven initiative providing sustainable water solutions to rural communities. Our mission is to empower women, children, and families and thus the whole community is enlivened through access to clean water, improving health, education, and economic opportunities.

“We want this project to serve as a model for other communities.”

Our Story

Initiated by John Odolon and Kevin Best but under implementation by the Odenaansan Project. WaterPlus's mission is to address the root cause(s) of water scarcity in rural communities; often, whether directly or in indirectly, this cause is colonization.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide communities with access to clean water, enhancing health and self-reliance. We do this through a combination of sustainable water infrastructure, community-driven initiatives, education, training, and economic empowerment.

Our Initiatives

Our latest initiative is bringing hope to Agereger, Uganda, where women and children have long struggled with water scarcity. Spearheaded by Odenaansan Canada and implemented by the WaterPlus team in Uganda, it aims to provide the community with clean, reliable water.
