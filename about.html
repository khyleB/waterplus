History

Initiated by John Odolon and Kevin Best but under implementation by the Odenaansan Project. John and Kevin met through a mutual friend and were called to work together on the application of the ICD approach in the global south. As this work is community centred, we consulted the traditional leadership of Agereger and they pointed to the water problem.

WaterPlus addresses the root causes of water scarcity in rural communities. Often, directly or in indirectly it is colonization Our collaborative approach brings together local leaders, organizations, and experts to suggest and support applicable sustainable water systems. We strive to do this with an indigenized perspective and employing indigenous methodology.
